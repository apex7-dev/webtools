Frequently Asked Questions

How does PDF compression work?

Our PDF compressor uses advanced algorithms to reduce file size by optimizing images, removing redundant data, and compressing the PDF structure. The level of compression can be adjusted to balance between file size and quality.

Is my PDF file secure?

Yes, your files are processed securely on our servers and automatically deleted after 1 hour. We don't store or share your documents with any third parties.

What's the maximum file size I can compress?

You can compress PDF files up to 100MB with our free service. For larger files, consider using our premium service.

Will compression affect the quality of my PDF?

With the "Low Compression" setting, quality loss is minimal. Higher compression levels may reduce image quality but preserve text clarity. You can preview the result before downloading.

Can I compress multiple PDFs at once?

Currently, our tool processes one file at a time. For batch processing, check out our premium PDF tools.
